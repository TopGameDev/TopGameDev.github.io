Background

My mother was an All-American basketball player when she was in college, so when my brother and I decided to play basketball she was thrilled. Why Web Development?

Creativity and Freedom, Jun 30, 2023

Why do I want to be a web developer? Well, that's simple. I like to build things and I'm an Artist at heart, I love to organize and re-arrange things to make them look Abstract, or mind bending and Opaque, or just down right cool. This type of work is right up my alley. Ive been designing since I was young. I also have always wanted to make my own website from scratch. I thought it was something that was beyond my capabilities when I was younger, then I came to find out in college that HTML is not as hard as I thought. I love the Freedom and Creativity that CSS brings to the web developing space. CSS is what I am most excited to learn about web development. Once I get a firm hold of the concepts and techniques I am going to make such cool and unique websites. To be able to land a job that is both code and art would be amazing to say the least.
